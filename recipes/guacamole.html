Guacamole recipe

Description

This is a recipe on preparation of guacamole.Preparation takes about 15 minutes.It is just a few ingredients away and you will be surprised how easy it is to make this great guacamole recipe.Step by step process on how to make yourself a guacamole dish is below.

ingredients

  • 3 avocados -peeled,pitted and mashed
  • 1 lime,juiced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 roma(plum)tomatoes diced
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 pinch ground cayenne pepper(optional)

Steps

  1. mash avocados
  2. mash it with lime juice and salt in a bowl
  3. mix in tomatoes,onion,cilantro and garlic
  4. stir in cayenne pepper
